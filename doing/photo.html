I run by the charles at least every week during the school year, for over two years now. It never bores me. It simply just has all the right combinations: grass, trees, ducks, Boston skyline, and other fit runners.

I was in seattle the summer of 2012 interning at Microsoft. I had a great time and made enough money to buy my Olympus OM-D EM5. The landscapes in Seattle was particularly inspiring in terms of teasing my desire to take photos. Things seemed to have more culture and meaning to them. Perhpas this is just my inability to capture complex messages in my photos.

New Bedford is a very soothing town. I went down to join Niamh, a great friend from England, for her visit to the States. The people were friendly and the ocean equally inviting. We went boating and scooter riding. I also got to meet John, who inexplicitly gave me many ideas to chew on about photography. His photos are great.
